# Hacker Lab setup

## Limited internet access

1. Configure your VMs for "Host Only" network access.
1. Use [mitmproxy](https://mitmproxy.org/) and [mitmproxy-block-traffic](https://github.com/fx2301/mitmproxy-block-traffic)
